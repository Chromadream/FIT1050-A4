Who we are

Founded in 2003, HARDCORE TANO*D! is one of earliest Japanese Hardcore group operating in Melbourne. Initially consisting of Yoshikazu Nagai, Tomoyuki Hamada, and arai, HARDCORE TANO*D! has gained followings from many Melbourne partygoers, and fans from around the world. Currently, HARDCORE TANO*D! has 12 members and continously growing to be the largest Japanese Hardcore collective.

What me make

On our musical journey, we decided to take inspiration from Japanese Hardcore music and denpa music, as it is seen to be highly compatible with Melbourne's fast-paced society, and we want to embody that spirit with the most enjoyable way possible.
