About Kawaiicore Collective!

Founded by the duo Megumi Kato and Utaha Kasumigaoka in 2014, Kawaiicore Collective! is one of the loudest J-Core collective in Melbourne, filling up festivals and venues on Melbourne and many other cities in Victoria.

Members

There are currently 4 members on Kawaiicore Collective! with their own distinctive style on J-Core, allowing us to be the most diversely-styled J-Core collective ever.
