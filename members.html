Members

The part of the Kawaiicore Collective! family.

Exvius

Exvius is a collaboration from the founders of Kawaiicore Collective!, Megumi Kato and Utaha Kasumigaoka. Prior to founding Kawaiicore Collective!, Megumi and Utaha is a prominent producer on the J-Core scene under the name of Mikuia. Since its conception, Exvius is a strictly-digital collaboration, never appearing most on festivals. With the signature fast-paced beats and high-pitched vocals, Exvius surely bring joy to large audiences.

DJ Nishikino

Part of Kawaiicore Collective! since late 2014, DJ Nishikino is a truly talented producer with his ability to drive the crowds with high-energy songs. He's also well-known by his unique twist to the Japanese Hardcore, creating the legendary EP that was still played on many venues, surippu EP, which was released by Kawaiicore Collective! in early 2015.

Camellia

Since becoming part of Kawaiicore Collective! in early 2016, Camellia never ceases to amaze everyone with his diverse styles of J-Core productions, creating some highly acclaimed J-Core songs, such as INSECTICIDE and crystallized. He is the lead producer on KAWAIICORE!Vol.2 album, and he has also released his solo EP titled CyphisoniaEP.

Yoshikazu Nagai

Being the latest addition of Kawaiicore Collective!, Yoshikazu Nagai is nothing short of a miracle, as he brings happiness to many people from his uplifting J-Core songs, but also manages to make people dance hard from his heavier works. Before joining Kawaiicore Collective!, Yoshikazu Nagai is a composer for Touhou Project music under the name SAISEN TURN. He is the lead producer of KAWAIICORE!Vol.3 album, and he also re-releases most of his older works on Kawaiicore Collective!
